Daiquiri

Why drink it?

You can drink Daiquiri when you cannot mock using your favorite cocktail.
For instance, you cannot mock InitialFactoryBuilder of JDK.

How do I drink it?

<dependency>
    <groupId>org.daiquiri</groupId>
    <artifactId>daiquiri</artifactId>
    <version>0.1.1</version>
    <scope>test</scope>
</dependency
